Our Process

Our Story

We moved from Kamalia to Lahore in 2013. One of the problems that We faced in Lahore was to get pure products like Butter and Desi Ghee as pure as in Kamalia. We used to bring these products with us from Kamalia to Lahore on weekly basis. We tried it from few places in Lahore but it had impurities and its taste was not even close to what it should taste. To ensure 100% quality, We built an animal farm in Kamalia from where we get pure milk and then we convert it into Butter and Desi Ghee. We believe there are lots of people in Lahore who are badly in need of pure products like these. This encouraged us to build DesiSawad.com where you can get 100% impurity free high quality Desi items like Desi Ghee and Butter.
